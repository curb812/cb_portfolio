Claire (Curb) Burke

Welcome to my portfolio site. I'm Claire, most often known as Curb, a designer and researcher.

About Me

I make complex information accessible and compelling for diverse audiences. My goal is to create new modes of interacting with information that are equitable and enhance user experiences. My background in research and design allows me to think both analytically and visually, melding information into a palatable, impactful whole.

I am an outdoor human of the bike-variety. I started with road biking, traversing the U.S. from New Hampshire to Washington. Shortly thereafter, I got hooked on mountain biking. I learned how to tango with rock and roots in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia, and have since made my way to Durango, Colorado, to explore Southwestern vistas, sedimentary slabs and high country traverses.

I am making the switch from environmental consulting to a design-focused career. I am expanding my skillset in UX/UI, CSS, HTML and Python, and seeking new opportunities in graphic and web design and/or technology.
